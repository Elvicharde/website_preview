Apple Pie Recipe

A yummy looking Apple pie

Apple pies are one of the most likely dessert options in a typical American home. They are tasty, nice, and have that homely vibe to them. "Hey, looks like its Apple Pie for dessert! Yay"

Ingredients

For a nicely done Apple Pie, you will need the following ingredients (in no particular order):

Double-Crust Pastry

  • 2 cups Gold Medal all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2/3 cup plus 2 tablespoons shortening
  • 4 to 6 tablespoons cold water

Filling

  • 1/3 to 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup Gold Medal all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 8 cups thinly sliced peeled tart apples (8 medium)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
